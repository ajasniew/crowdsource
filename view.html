How to use Crowdsourcing Platform in Computational Pathology?



What is crowdsourcing? What is CrowdFlower?

Crowdsourcing is the outsourcing of tasks typically performed by a few experts to a large crowd. CrowdFlower is crowdsourcing platform.

Image Analysis - Classification of Regions

In this task CrowdFlower contributors identify the primary characteristic of various images of histological regions.
Pathological diagnoses are based on visual characteristics of tissues.
Different histologic subtypes in lung adenocarcinomas can be classified by the predominant pattern of growth.

Image Analysis - Cell Nuclei Classification

In this task CrowdFlower contributors identify all of the morphological characteristics of the cell nuclei in an image.

Perinuclear halos - refers to the presence of a vacuolated area that surrounds the nucleus (a clear area around the nucleus). It results from nuclear shrinking.

Image Annotation - Nuclei Segmentation

In this task CrowdFlower contributors draw boundary of nuclei where they are found in the image.

Image Annotation - Nuclei Detection

In this task CrowdFlower contributors place a dot at the center of nuclei where they are found in the image.
