How to use Crowdsourcing Platform in Computational Pathology?



What is crowdsourcing? What is CrowdFlower?

Crowdsourcing is the outsourcing of tasks typically performed by a few experts to a large crowd. CrowdFlower is crowdsourcing platform.

Image Analysis - Classification of Regions

In this task CrowdFlower contributors will identify the primary characteristic of various images of histological regions.

Image Analysis - Cell Nuclei Classification

In this task CrowdFlower contributors will identify all of the morphological characteristics of the cell nuclei in an image.

Image Annotation - Nuclei Segmentation

In this task CrowdFlower contributors will draw boundary of nuclei where they are found in the image.

Image Annotation - Nuclei Detection

In this task CrowdFlower contributors will place a dot at the center of nuclei where they are found in the image.
